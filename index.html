The project

We all know how much data NASA collects daily about our planet, but where is this information? The goal of the Holomap project is to facilitate education about our atmosphere linked to the terrestrial ecosystem, making it fun and interesting for the public. Our platform is based on the tools offered on the Worldwind Platform, but with full focus on the educational environment. How do we do it? The project
